Solar Panels

A common misconception is that people mix up solar panels and solar modules. A solar module is one plate of solar cells, a solar panel (also called photo-voltaic module) is a bunch of solar modules put next to each other. When sunlight lands on the solar cells of a solar module, the solar cell turns the photons from the sunlight into electricity.
